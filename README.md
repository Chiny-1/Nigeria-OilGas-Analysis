## 💡 Key Findings
- Nigeria's peak production was **2,695 thousand B/D** in 2005
- Nigeria's worst period was **1981-1988** (8 consecutive Bust years)
- Nigeria's best decade was the **2000s** (avg 2,293 thousand B/D)
- Nigeria ranks **6th** among OPEC members
- Saudi Arabia produces approximately **4x more** than Nigeria
- Nigeria's OPEC share has declined from **7.14%** to **6.53%**
- Pipeline security is the **key driver** of Nigeria's production cycles
